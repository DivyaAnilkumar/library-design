The Hobbit

Summary

"The Hobbit" by J.R.R. Tolkien is a fantasy novel that follows the adventures of Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit from the Shire who is reluctantly drawn into an epic quest. Bilbo's peaceful life is disrupted when the wizard Gandalf and a group of dwarves led by Thorin Oakenshield arrive at his doorstep, seeking his help in reclaiming their homeland from the dragon Smaug. Despite his initial reluctance, Bilbo joins the group on a perilous journey across Middle-earth. Throughout their travels, Bilbo and the company face numerous challenges, including trolls, goblins, giant spiders, and elves. One of the pivotal moments of the journey occurs when Bilbo finds a magical ring that grants him the power of invisibility. This ring becomes crucial in helping the group navigate their dangers and ultimately confront Smaug. The story culminates in the Battle of Five Armies, where various factions clash over the treasure hoard of the Lonely Mountain.
